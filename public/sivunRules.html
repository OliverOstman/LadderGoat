The six commandments of the laddergoat

  1. The main point is to upload pictures and videos of funny gaming moments (glitches, bugs, fails and other funny stuff).
  2. Do not upload anything else. As in non-gaming stuff.
  3. If you find something funny in a game chances are someone else will find it funny too.
  4. comment rules:
    1. Do not bully other people.
    2. No racist comments.
    3. No sexist comments.
    4. Respect others opinions.
    5. Do not make others uncomfortable.
  5. Must be over 15 years old some content might contain imagery that is not suited for little kids.
  6. Remember to have fun and enjoy.
